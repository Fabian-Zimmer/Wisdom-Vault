# Broccoli in Garlic Sauce

## Ingredients (for ~400g broccoli)

**Note:** Scale ingredients accordingly if making for larger amount of broccoli.

| Amount  | Name                            | Prep                                   |
| ------- | ------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| 1 big   | Broccoli                        | ~400g, separated into medium florets   |
| 2-3     | Garlic cloves                   | Crushed and finely chopped             |
|         | Ginger                          | Equal amount as garlic, finely chopped |
| 1 tbsp  | Light soy sauce                 |                                        |
| 1 tsp   | Dark soy sauce                  |                                        |
| 1/2 tsp | Brown sugar or jaggery          |                                        |
| 1/4 tsp | Sesame oil                      |                                        |
| 1 tsp   | Potato starch                   |                                        |
| 2 tbsp  | Water                           |                                        |
| 2 tsp   | Chilli oil                      |                                        |
|         | White and/or black sesame seeds | For finishing                          |

## Instructions

1. Boil broccoli florets (1 big/~400g) in water for approximately 3-4 minutes maximum. The florets should have a heavy bite as the wok session will cook them some more.
2. Prepare the sauce by mixing light soy sauce (1 tbsp), dark soy sauce (1 tsp), brown sugar or jaggery (1/2 tsp), sesame oil (1/4 tsp), potato starch (1 tsp), and water (2 tbsp).
3. In a wok, heat chilli oil (2 tsp).
4. Add crushed and finely chopped garlic (2-3 cloves) and finely chopped ginger (equal amount as garlic). Sauté for approximately 3 minutes.
5. Add boiled broccoli and sauté for 1 minute.
6. Add the sauce, cover the wok, and steam for approximately 1 minute.
7. Sprinkle generous amount of white and/or black sesame seeds.

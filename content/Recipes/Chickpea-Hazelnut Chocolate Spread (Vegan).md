## Ingredients

| Amount          | Name                 | Prep             |
| --------------- | -------------------- | ---------------- |
| 1/2 can (~125g) | Cooked chickpeas     | Drained & rinsed |
| 1/4 cup (~30g)  | Hazelnuts            |                  |
| 6 big           | Medjool dates        | Pitted           |
| 1/4 cup (~30g)  | Dutched cocoa powder |                  |
| ~180ml          | Water                |                  |
| ~40g            | Vegan butter         |                  |
| ~10g            | Agave                |                  |

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 200C.
	- (optional step, had mixed tasting results): Put the cooked and rinsed chickpeas (1/2 can | ~125g) together with the hazelnuts (1/4 cup | ~30g) on a baking tray and roast in oven at 200C for ~25 minutes.
	- Otherwise just add to container directly to blend.
2. Put the (optionally roasted) chickpeas and hazelnuts in a container and mix (e.g. with staff blender) together with pitted Medjool dates (6 big), dutched cocoa powder (1/4 cup | ~30g), and water (~180 ml) together in bowl.
3. Transfer to appropriate container and store in fridge.

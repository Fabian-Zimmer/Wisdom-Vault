### Base

| Amount | Name        | Prep |
| ------ | ----------- | ---- |
| 60g    | Almonds     | Total, do in 2 batches |
| 80g    | Rolled oats | Total, do in 2 batches |
| 60g    | Oil         | Total, do in 2 batches |
| 60g    | Dates       | Total, do in 2 batches |

### Filling

| Amount   | Name                  | Prep                |
| -------- | --------------------- | ------------------- |
| 2 cups   | Cashew nuts           | Boiled              |
| 400g     | Cooked chickpeas      | Canned, drained and rinsed |
| 1 tsp    | Vanilla extract       |                     |
| 400g     | Coconut milk          | Canned              |
| 1/3 cup  | Maple syrup           |                     |
| 2 tbsp   | Almond or cashew butter |                   |
| 2        | Lemons                | Zest of both, juice of half |
| 1/4 tsp  | Salt                  |                     |

## Instructions

1. For the base, blend almonds (30g), rolled oats (40g), oil (30g), and dates (30g) in a small food processor. Repeat with the second batch of the same amounts (total: 60g almonds, 80g oats, 60g oil, 60g dates).
2. Spread the base mixture and make walls in a 28cm diameter round baking form.
3. Bake at 180°C for 15 minutes.
4. During the same 15 minutes, boil cashew nuts (2 cups) in a pot of water.
5. Put everything in a large container and blend until smooth: boiled and drained cashew nuts (2 cups), drained and rinsed cooked chickpeas (400g can), vanilla extract (1 tsp), coconut milk (400g can), maple syrup (1/3 cup), almond or cashew butter (2 tbsp), zest of lemons (2), juice from half of one lemon, and salt (1/4 tsp).
6. Pour the filling into the oven form, smooth and flatten the top, then bake at 180°C for ~35 minutes.
7. Best served with mashed and hot berries topping.

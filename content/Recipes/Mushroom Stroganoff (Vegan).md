## Instructions

1. Chop mushrooms (~550-600g total: brown Champignons (250g), brown Shitake (150g), white Oyster mushrooms (150g)) and leeks (2 large).

2. Cook in two batches (half of each ingredient per batch): First, cook mushrooms and leeks for approximately 10 minutes.

3. Add garlic cloves (6, chopped), dried thyme (1 tsp), and salt (1/2 tsp) to the mushrooms and cook for 2-3 minutes.

4. While the mushrooms cook, prepare vegetable broth (360ml, half cube) and stir in all-purpose flour (30g) and soy sauce (2 tbsp).

5. Cook pasta (350g) according to package instructions.

6. After completing the second batch of mushrooms, deglaze with white wine (50ml) and let it cook away for approximately 2-3 minutes.

7. Add the broth mixture, coconut milk (400ml/1 can), tahini (2 tbsp), nutritional yeast (2 tbsp), and red chilli (or paprika) powder (1 tsp). Simmer for approximately 10 minutes.

8. Finish by stirring in Dijon mustard (1/2 tsp), fresh dille (~7.5g/handful/1/4 cup or dried), and cooked pasta (350g).

9. Top with freshly cracked black pepper and serve.

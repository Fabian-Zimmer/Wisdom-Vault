## Ingredients

| Amount | Name | Prep |
| ------ | ---- | ---- |
| 2.5 cups | All purpose flour | |
| 2 tbsp | Sugar | |
| 1/4 cup | Sunflower oil | |
| 25g | Vegan butter | Directly from fridge |
| 1 tsp | Baking powder | |
| Pinch | Salt | |
| ~140ml | Oat milk | Unsweetened, no barista |

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, sunflower oil, and cold vegan butter. Mix until the mixture becomes flaky.
2. Add baking powder and salt, then mix to incorporate.
3. Gradually add oat milk bit by bit until the dough reaches a semi-soft texture.
4. Without refrigerating, roll out the dough into a disk approximately 32cm in diameter.
5. Place into a 28-32cm form and trim excess dough. Use a fork to poke many holes into the base.
6. Bake at 180°C for approximately 15 minutes.

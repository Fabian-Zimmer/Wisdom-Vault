## Ingredients (for 6 muffins)

| Amount              | Name                                          | Prep              |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| 1/2 large           | Ripe banana                                   | Mashed            |
| 1/2                 | Lemon                                         | Zest and juice    |
| 1/6 cup (~2.5 tbsp) | Almond milk                                   |                   |
| 1/4 cup (~4 tbsp)   | Greek yogurt<br>(or coconut yogurt for vegan) |                   |
| 1.5 tbsp            | Maple syrup                                   |                   |
| 1 tbsp              | Coconut oil                                   | Melted/liquid     |
| 1/2 tsp             | Vanilla extract                               |                   |
| 1/2 cup (max.)      | Blueberries                                   | Don't squish them |
| 1 tbsp              | Poppy seeds                                   |                   |
| 38g                 | Almond flour                                  |                   |
| 90g                 | Fine grained wheat or spelt flour             |                   |
| 1 tsp               | Baking powder                                 |                   |

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
2. In a bowl, add wet ingredients in order, mixing as you go: mashed ripe banana (1/2 large), lemon zest (1/2 lemon), lemon juice (1/2 lemon), almond milk (1/6 cup/2.5 tbsp), coconut yogurt (1/4 cup/4 tbsp), maple syrup (1.5 tbsp — no less, otherwise flour taste is too strong), melted coconut oil (1 tbsp), and vanilla extract (1/2 tsp).
3. Add blueberries (1/2 cup max. — don't squish them) and poppy seeds (1 tbsp).
4. In a separate bowl, weigh and mix together almond flour (38g), fine grained flour (90g), and baking powder (1 tsp).
5. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture in the main bowl and mix until just combined.
6. Reduce oven temperature to 175°C and divide batter into 6-muffin-oven-form.
7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until the tops are a good brown color.

**Note:** Too many blueberries will release water and mess up proper cooking, so use max. 1/2 cup.

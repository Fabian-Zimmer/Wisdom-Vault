## Ingredients

| Amount  | Name              | Prep                                             |
| ------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| 1 large | Zucchini          | Cut into medium-thin slices                      |
| 2 tbsp  | Olive oil         |                                                  |
| 1 tbsp  | Anis seeds        |                                                  |
| 1       | Quiche/Tart dough | To self-make use [[Quiche & Tart Dough (Vegan)]] |
| 200g    | Ricotta           |                                                  |
| 200g    | Feta              | Crumbled with fingers                            |
| Handful | Mint leaves       | Cut in fine pieces                               |
|         | Black pepper      | Freshly cracked, lots                            |
| Dash    | Salt              |                                                  |
| 40g     | Cashew nuts       | For egg replacement                              |
| 50g     | Water             | For egg replacement                              |
| 2 tsp   | Agar-agar powder  | For egg replacement                              |
|         | (Lingonberry) Jam | To serve with                                    |

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
2. Cook sliced zucchinis in a pot with olive oil and anis seeds for 5-10 minutes until soft.
3. Prepare flat dough in a 28cm oven form and bake for 15 minutes at 180°C until the bottom is slightly brown.
4. For the egg replacement, blend cashew nuts, water, and agar-agar powder together until smooth.
5. In a bowl, combine ricotta, feta crumbled with fingers, mint leaves, black pepper, and salt. Mix well.
6. Add the egg replacement to the ricotta mixture and mix thoroughly.
7. Pour the mixture into the prepared oven form, then arrange the cooked zucchini slices in overlapping circles on top.
8. Bake at 180°C for 45-60 minutes.

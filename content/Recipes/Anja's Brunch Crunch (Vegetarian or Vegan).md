## Ingredients

| Amount    | Name                                                                       | Prep                               |
| --------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| 1 cup     | Almonds & Hazelnut shredded mix                                            |                                    |
| 1 tbsp    | Brown sugar                                                                |                                    |
| 1 big     | Apple                                                                      | grated                             |
| 2 big     | Bananas                                                                    | mashed                             |
| 1 cup     | Plant-based milk                                                           | oat > almond > soy (my preference) |
| 1/2 cup   | (rolled) Oat flakes                                                        |                                    |
| 1/4 cup   | Chia seeds                                                                 |                                    |
| 1/4 cup   | Flaxseed powder                                                            |                                    |
| By choice | Plant-based yogurt like soy (for #Vegan) or Greek yogurt (for #Vegetarian) |                                    |

## Instructions

0. (if not available) make a half-half almond-hazelnut shredded mix by blending in mixer of with staff mixer.
1. Soak (rolled) oat flakes (1/2 cup), Chia seeds (1/4 cup), and flaxseed powder (1/4 cup) in plant-based milk (1 cup)
2. Mix the almond-hazelnut shredded mix (1 cup) with brown sugar (1 tbsp), then heat in pan together, stirring and turning it over frequently to avoid burning, until slightly brown and clumpy.
3. Grate apple (1 big) and mash bananas (2 big).
4. Fully mix the apple-banana paste together with the soaked ingredients. Pour nut mixture evenly on top to cover and don't mix for aesthetics.
5. Eat with yogurt (plant-based for #Vegan or Greek for #Vegetarian).

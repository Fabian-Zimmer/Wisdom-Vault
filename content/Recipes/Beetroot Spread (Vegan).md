## Ingredients

| Amount         | Name             | Prep              |
| -------------- | ---------------- | ----------------- |
| 4 medium-sized | Beetroots        | oven-baked        |
| 2 small-medium | Garlic cloves    |                   |
| 1 tbsp         | Olive oil        |                   |
| 1/2 cup        | Hemp hearts      |                   |
| 1/4 cup        | Water            |                   |
| 1/4 cup        | White tahini     |                   |
| 2 tbsp         | Lemon/lime juice | Half a lemon/lime |
| 1 tbsp         | Balsamic vinegar |                   |
| 1/2 tsp        | Salt             |                   |
| 5-6 turns      | Black pepper     | Freshly cracked   |

## Instructions

1. Wrap the 4 beetroots separately in aluminium foil. Bake in an oven bowl at 210C for 60-70min.
2. Blend all ingredients together using a blender or staff mixer: oven-baked beets (4 medium-sized beetroots), garlic cloves (2 medium-sized), olive oil (1 tbsp), hemp hearts (1/2 cup), water (1/4 cup), white tahini (1/4 cup), lemon/lime juice (2 tbsp), balsamic vinegar (1 tbsp), salt (1/2 tsp), and freshly cracked black pepper (5-6 turns) until smooth.

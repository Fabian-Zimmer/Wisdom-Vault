## Instructions

**Note:** Make rice or rotis for serving.

1. In a big pot with olive oil, cook red onion rings (2 big), chopped green chillies (3-4), chopped garlic cloves (4), and chopped ginger (as much as garlic).
2. When slightly brown, add roughly chopped tomatoes (4-5 big, ~500g), Medjool date (1/2-1), and cashew nuts (10-15). Cook until soft (~5-10 mins.).
3. Take off heat purée the mixture (e.g., with staff mixer).
4. In a wok, activate Khade Masale (whole spices) with some olive oil: bay leaves (2), black cardamom (2), green cardamom (4-5), pepper corns (4-5), cinnamon (1 inch piece).
5. In a separate pot, start cooking the peas (1-1.5 cup) on medium heat. 
6. After some time, add the puréed mixture, Garam masala (2 tsp), red chilli powder (1-1.5 tsp), turmeric (1/2 tsp), coriander powder (1 tsp), jeera powder (1/2 tsp), and salt (start with 1 tsp, adjust to taste later). 
7. Cut and add paneer (2 packs/600-800g).
8. Add a little bit of water and a little bit of soya cuisine until desired consistency.
9. Add peas when cooked enough, and adjust salt to taste. 
10. Top with chopped coriander leaves (~15g, small bunch/pack) and serve with rice or rotis.

## Ingredients

| Amount | Name | Prep |
| ------ | ---- | ---- |
| 1kg | Potatoes | Skin on |
| 200g | Grated cheese | Gruyère + Parmesan |
| 4-5 | Garlic cloves | Finely sliced |
| 1 big | Leek | Finely chopped |
| 1 big | Zucchini | Thin slices |
| 200g | Spinach | |
| ~300ml | Soya cuisine | |
| | Black pepper | To taste |
| | Salt | To taste |
| | Oil | For baking tray |

## Instructions

1. Pressure cook potatoes with skin on for 1 whistle.
2. In a wok, cook garlic, leek, and zucchini until softened.
3. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
4. Slice the cooked potatoes thinly.
5. Oil a large oven baking tray.
6. Create alternating layers of sliced potatoes, vegetable mixture, and grated cheese.
7. Pour soya cuisine throughout all layers, adding pepper and salt to each layer.
8. Bake until golden and bubbling, 30-40 mins.

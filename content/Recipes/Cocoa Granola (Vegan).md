# Cocoa Granola

## Ingredients

| Amount         | Name                 | Prep                           |
| -------------- | -------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| 40g (~1/6 cup) | Honey                |                                |
| 75g (~1/3 cup) | Coconut oil          |                                |
| 2 cups (~160g) | Rolled oats          |                                |
| 2 cups (total) | Seeds and nuts       | See suggestions in next 4 rows |
| e.g., 1/2 cup  | Almonds              | Cut in thirds                  |
| e.g., 1/2 cup  | Macadamias           | Cut in halves                  |
| e.g., 1/2 cup  | Coconut flakes       |                                |
| e.g., 1/2 cup  | Pumpkin seeds        |                                |
| 1/4 cup        | Dutched cocoa powder |                                |

## Instructions

1. Put a small pot on a weighing scale and add honey (40g/~1/6 cup) and coconut oil (75g/~1/3 cup). Melt on low heat.
2. In a large bowl, combine rolled oats (2 cups/~160g), seeds and nuts (2 cups total — favorite combination: almonds (1/2 cup, cut in thirds), macadamias (1/2 cup, cut in halves), coconut flakes (1/2 cup), pumpkin seeds (1/2 cup)), and dutched cocoa powder (1/4 cup).
3. Pour in the honey and oil mixture, then mix all well together.
4. Spread and flatten the mixture onto a baking tray lined with baking paper.
5. Bake at 150°C for ~15 minutes, flip everything over, then bake for another ~15 minutes (approximately 30 minutes total).

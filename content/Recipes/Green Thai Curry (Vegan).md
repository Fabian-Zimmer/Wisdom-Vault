# Vegan Green Thai Curry

## Ingredients

| Amount      | Name                    | Prep                          |
| ----------- | ----------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| 1 cup       | Jasmine rice            |                               |
| ~350-400g   | Firm tofu               | Cut in small rectangles       |
|             | Sunflower oil           | For frying                    |
| 2 cans      | Coconut milk            | 400ml each                    |
| 685ml       | Water                   |                               |
| 1           | Red bell pepper         | Sliced or chopped finely      |
| 1/2         | Eggplant                | Sliced or chopped finely      |
| 1 medium    | Broccoli                | Sliced or chopped finely      |
| 4-5         | Garlic cloves           | Thinly sliced                 |
|             | Ginger                  | Same volume as garlic, grated |
| 2 stalks    | Lemongrass              | Thinly sliced                 |
| 3           | Green chillies          | Thinly sliced                 |
| 4-5 tbsp    | Thai green curry paste  | ~170g                         |
| 2 tsp       | Coconut/brown sugar     |                               |
| 1/2         | Lime                    | Juiced                        |
| 1 tsp       | Salt                    |                               |
| ~10         | Thai Basil leaves       |                               |

## Instructions

1. Cook jasmine rice (1 cup).

2. Fry firm tofu (~350-400g, cut in small rectangles) in a wok with sunflower oil until golden. Set aside.

3. In the wok, dissolve the solid coconut cream from coconut milk cans (2 cans, 400ml each).

4. Add thinly sliced garlic (4-5 cloves), grated ginger (same volume as garlic), thinly sliced lemongrass (2 stalks), thinly sliced green chillies (3), Thai green curry paste (4-5 tbsp/~170g), coconut/brown sugar (2 tsp), and lime juice (1/2 lime). Cook until fragrant.

5. Add the remaining liquid coconut milk from the cans and water (685ml). Bring to a boil.

6. Add eggplant (1/2, sliced or chopped), red bell pepper (1, sliced or chopped), and salt (1 tsp). Let cook for 7-10 minutes until vegetables are tender/al dente.

7. Add broccoli (1 medium, sliced or chopped) and fried tofu.

8. Add Thai Basil leaves (~10), turn off heat, and let rest for approximately 10 minutes.

9. Serve with jasmine rice.
